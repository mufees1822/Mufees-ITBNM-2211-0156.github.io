About Our Hotel

Welcome to Lanka Hotel, where luxury meets comfort. Discover our story and what makes us unique.

Our History

Lanka Hotel was founded in 2022 with a vision to provide unmatched hospitality and an exceptional experience to every guest. Nestled in the heart of Kandy, our hotel has become a landmark of elegance and comfort.

From humble beginnings, we have grown to become one of the most esteemed hotels in the region, known for our dedication to quality, personalized service, and attention to detail.

Our Mission & Values

At Lanka Hotel, our mission is to provide a sanctuary for our guests, where they can relax, rejuvenate, and enjoy the finest amenities and services. Our core values of hospitality, excellence, and integrity are at the heart of everything we do.
